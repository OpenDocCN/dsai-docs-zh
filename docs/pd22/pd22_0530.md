```py
Period.day
```

获取 Period 所在月份的日期。

返回：

int

[`Period.dayofweek`](https://pandas.pydata.org/docs/reference/api/pandas.Period.dayofweek.html#pandas.Period.dayofweek "pandas.Period.dayofweek")

示例

```py
>>> p = pd.Period("2018-03-11", freq='h')
```
